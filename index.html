M.Sc. Soil Science Student | Graduate Research Assistant | University of Manitoba
Precision 4R management, nitrogen use efficiency, and greenhouse gas emissions in canola systems

Rijwan Sai is an M.Sc. Soil Science student and Graduate Research Assistant in the Department of Soil Science at the University of Manitoba, Canada. His current research focuses on Precision 4R management to improve nitrogen use efficiency and reduce greenhouse gas emissions in canola production systems. He has research experience spanning soil fertility, crop productivity, fertiliser management, and sustainable agriculture, with publications and presentations across multiple agricultural topics.

Academic background
